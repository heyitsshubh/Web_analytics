Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately.

What counts as an "event"?

Every page view and click tracked by the tracker.js script counts as one event. Dashboard views and API calls do not count.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Every paid plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact support for a full refund.
